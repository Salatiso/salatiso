About Salatiso

Risk expert, author, website developer, and father—blending professional excellence with storytelling and advocacy for family and culture.

Explore My Work

Professional Career

Over 20 years in risk management, compliance, and OHS, with roles at Barclays Africa, Liberty Group, and Gyro Group. I design EHS programs, conduct audits, and hold certifications in Cybersecurity and AI & Law (Coursera, 2023-2024). Founder of SafetyHelp, delivering automated OHS solutions.

Website Development

I craft functional, purpose-driven websites. Notable projects include flamea.org for fathers’ rights and sazi.life for Xhosa culture preservation. Upcoming: Safety First Tools with a Health and Safety Chatbot.

Authorship

Author of Goliath’s Reckoning and The Perfect Cage series, I write to challenge biases and inspire change. My works, available on Google Play and Apple Books, blend advocacy and storytelling. Visit the Books page for more.

Personal Life

A dedicated father homeschooling my son, Sazi, through hands-on DIY projects. I share insights on YouTube (@salatiso) and advocate for fathers via Flamea. Also a property entrepreneur in Johannesburg South.
