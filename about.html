Who I Am

I’m Salatiso Mdeni, a South African author, risk pro, and founder of flamea.org. I’ve spent 20+ years tackling challenges in banking and transport, but my real passion is writing—calling out injustices, empowering dads, and dreaming up bold futures.

My Mission: To fight for fairness and family, from courtrooms to classrooms, through flamea.org and my books.

My Roots: LLB (almost there), B-Tech Environmental Health (2008). Advisor (2023–Present), ex-Gyro Group (2016–2022).

My Life: Homeschooling my kids, diving into AI, and chatting in English, Nguni, and Sotho.
