About Me

I’m Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni, a South African with a rich tapestry of experiences—risk and compliance expert, author, father, and DIY enthusiast. This page reflects my journey, blending professional expertise with personal passions, shaped by my family and heritage. From managing complex risks to writing transformative stories, and nurturing my kids through homeschooling, I strive to inspire and connect with both my professional network and personal circle.

Professional Career Timeline (Highlights)

  • 2003-2006: Early career in Public Health and Occupational Hygiene at the Department of Health and Anglo Platinum.
  • 2007-2010: Progressed into management roles in Safety, Health & Environment at SARCC Metrorail and Liberty Group Properties.
  • 2010-2016: Specialized in Risk, Compliance & Audit at Barclays Africa, also establishing 5G Property Consultants.
  • 2016-2024: Further specialization in Risk, Audit, Business Continuity & Compliance at Gyro Group.
  • 2023-Present: Dedicated to Authorship, Writing, Publishing, and Advisory roles in Risk, Compliance, Health and Safety.
  • Current Focus: Leveraging expertise at Safetyhelp.co to provide OHS expertise and tools.
