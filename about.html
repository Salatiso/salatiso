Who I Am

I’m Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni, a South African author, risk pro, and founder of flamea.org. I’ve spent 20+ years tackling challenges, but my real passion is writing—calling out injustices, empowering dads, and dreaming up bold futures.

My Mission: To fight for fairness and family, from courtrooms to classrooms, through flamea.org and my books.

My Roots: I earned a degree in Environmental Health and nearly finished an LLB, dropping out with just three modules left out of principle—I couldn’t join a system I see as flawed.

My Life: Homeschooling my kids, diving into AI, and chatting in English, Nguni, and Sotho. I’m a father to my son, with all else a means to this end.
