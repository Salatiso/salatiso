Early Influences (1982-1999)

Excelled at Kiriyatswane Secondary School, earning awards in poetry, physics, Xhosa, and biology. An early interest in health and safety emerged from observing industrial impacts near Mbalenhle township.

×

Education & Early Career (2000-2008)

Earned N. Dip and B-Tech in Environmental Health at TUT, focusing on occupational hygiene. Worked as a Junior Environmental Health Practitioner and excelled as a Ventilation and Occupational Hygiene Trainee at Anglo Platinum, named best trainee.

×

Mid-Career Growth (2007-2016)

Led SHE programs at SARCC Metrorail and Liberty Group Properties, revamping safety initiatives. As an EHS Consultant at Barclays Africa, developed a national asbestos program and coordinated evacuations.

×

Entrepreneurial Path (2010-Present)

Founded 5G Property Consultants for EHS and property management consultancy. Launched SafetyHelp for OHS compliance, Sazi for education, and Flamea for equality, alongside authoring OHS books.
