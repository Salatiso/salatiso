About Me

I’m Salatiso Mdeni, a South African author and risk management expert with 20+ years in banking, real estate, and transport. My books tackle tough issues—court biases, race policies, digital dystopias—while celebrating family and resilience.

Mission: To amplify voices through advocacy and storytelling, from fathers’ rights to sci-fi epics.

Background: LLB (near completion), B-Tech Environmental Health (2008). Advisor (2023–Present), Gyro Group Specialist (2016–2022).

Passions: Homeschooling, AI, and writing in English, Nguni, and Sotho.
