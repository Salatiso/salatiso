About Me

I’m Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni, a South African with a rich tapestry of experiences—risk and compliance expert, author, father, and DIY enthusiast. This page reflects my journey, blending professional expertise with personal passions, shaped by my family and heritage. From managing complex risks to writing transformative stories, and nurturing my kids through homeschooling, I strive to inspire and connect with both my professional network and personal circle.

Professional Career

With over 20 years of experience, I specialize in risk management, compliance, business continuity, occupational health and safety (OHS), and auditing. My career spans roles at Gyro Group, Barclays Africa, Liberty Group, and more, where I’ve developed and implemented EHS programs, conducted audits, and earned certifications from Coursera (e.g., Cybersecurity, AI & Law) and institutions like Pretoria Technikon, UNISA, TUT, GIBS and more.

  • Risk Management (Operational & Enterprise)
  • Compliance Management (Regulatory, POPI, Anti-Corruption)
  • Emergency Response Management (ERP Development, Implementation, Testing)
  • Business Continuity Management (BCP Development, Implementation, Testing)
  • Auditing (Planning, Coordination, Reporting)
  • EHS/ Asbestos/ Construction Management (Design, Execution, Programs)
  • Occupational Hygiene and Environmental Management
  • Project and Stakeholder Management
  • Recent Certifications: Cybersecurity, AI & Law (2023-2024)

Authorship
