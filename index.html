My Journey

My path has been one of continuous learning, adaptation, and a deep-seated drive to make a meaningful impact. From humble beginnings in rural Transkei to navigating complex risk landscapes, my experiences have shaped my perspective. My career began in Occupational Health and Safety (OHS), evolving into broader expertise in Risk Management and Compliance.

My Drive & Motivation

At the heart of everything is my role as a father: "I am a father to my son; all else is a means to this end." This extends to my responsibilities as an Umlandeli (successor) to my father, Mlandeli Nelson Mdeni, upholding our Xhosa family legacy. This includes being an uncle, a father-figure, a dutiful son, and provider. The names in our lineage – Mlandeli (successor), Salatiso (guide), and Sazisi (introducer, for my son Sazi, "Baza Kusazi!") – reflect a legacy of guidance and responsibility. This commitment fuels my passion for education, self-sufficiency, and equality.

Timeline of Key Milestones

1982 - Early Years

Raised in rural Transkei (Fort Malan, Nqabane, Songwevu, Mahasane). Early education often under a tree or in community churches. Key locations: Fort Malan (birthplace vicinity: 32°09'03"S 28°31'25"E), Nqabane (father's home: 31°55'37"S 28°21'40"E), Mahasane (first school: 32°20'10"S 28°43'35"E), Lencane (rented home: 32°08'30"S 28°19'55"E; Lencane Primary: 32°08'41"S 28°20'30"E), Drayini (childhood home: 32°08'09"S 28°20'39"E), Matolweni School (approx. 32°08'13"S 28°20'39"E).

1999

Matriculated from Kiriyatswane Secondary School, Mbalenhle, Mpumalanga. Excelled academically, earning awards in poetry, physics, Xhosa, and biology. Developed an early interest in health and safety.

2000 - 2008

National Diploma in Environmental Health, TUT (2002). Served as student representative. B-Tech in Environmental Health, TUT (2008). MDP, GIBS (2008). Numerous OHS and Risk certifications.

2003

Junior Environmental Health Practitioner. Conducted inspections, community education, research on informal vendor hygiene.

2004-2006

Ventilation & Occupational Hygiene Trainee, then Technologist. Awarded "Best Trainee" (2004). Acted in senior roles.

2007-2009

SHE Manager. Revamped programs, established occupational hygiene, led national rollout.

2008-2012

Founder & Project Manager. Awarded academic excellence in Lencane and Matolweni schools.

2009-2010

EHS Manager. Addressed DoL investigation post-incident, implemented robust OHS systems. Headhunted by ABSA.

2010-2016

EHS Consultant & Risk Specialist. Developed national asbestos program. Coordinated safe evacuations.

2012-2022

Managing Director. Consultancy for clients like Ster-Kinekor, Mine Investment Corp, Alexander Forbes.

2016-2024

Specialist: Risk, Audit, Business Continuity. Instrumental in designing compliance structures. Led risk management.

2023-Present

Creating e-learning content, advising on risk/compliance. Authored "Safety First" series.
