My Journey

My path has been one of continuous learning, adaptation, and a deep-seated drive to make a meaningful impact. From humble beginnings in rural Transkei, where my earliest education unfolded under a tree, to navigating complex risk landscapes for major corporations, my experiences have shaped my perspective and fueled my endeavors. My career began in Occupational Health and Safety (OHS), gradually evolving into a broader expertise in Risk Management, Compliance, and Business Continuity.

My Drive & Motivation

At the heart of everything I do is my role as a father: "I am a father to my son; all else is a means to this end." This core principle extends to my broader family responsibilities. As an Umlandeli (successor) to my father, Mlandeli Nelson Mdeni, I carry the duty to uphold our family's legacy and honor – a responsibility deeply embedded in Xhosa culture where a successor inherits not just assets but the mantle of familial and spiritual duties. This role transcends mere inheritance, emphasizing the continuation of lineage and the holistic well-being of the family, even if it requires personal sacrifice to address past debts and maintain family honor.

This commitment includes being an uncle to my nephews and nieces, a father-figure within my family, a dutiful son, and a provider for my mother and sisters. The significance of names in our lineage further underscores this: my grandfather named my father Mlandeli (the follower/successor); my father, in turn, named me Salatiso (show us the way/the guide); and I named my son Sazisi (the one who makes known/introduces), often called Sazi, embodying the spirit of "Baza Kusazi!" (They will know you!). This intergenerational naming reflects a legacy of guidance, responsibility, and the aspiration for each generation to illuminate the path for the next. This commitment to family and legacy fuels my passion for education, self-sufficiency, and creating a better future. I strive to share knowledge, empower others, and advocate for equality, driven by the resilience forged from overcoming challenges and a desire to leave a lasting positive impact.

1982 - Early Years

Born in Transkei, Eastern Cape

Raised in rural Transkei, my early education was unconventional, often taking place under a tree. This fostered resilience and a profound love for learning. My parents instilled a strong value for education; my mother’s self-sufficiency and my father’s emphasis on personal excellence were formative.

1999 - Secondary Education

Kiriyatswane Secondary School

Excelled academically, earning awards in poetry, physics, Xhosa, and biology. An early interest in health and safety was sparked by environmental concerns near a Sasol plant in Mbalenhle township.

2000 - 2008 - Tertiary Education & Certifications

Tshwane University of Technology (TUT) & GIBS
  • National Diploma in Environmental Health, TUT (2002).
  • Nominated and served as a student representative for my environmental health class, organizing activities and coordinating events.
  • B-Tech in Environmental Health, TUT (2008), focusing on occupational hygiene with practical training in Namibia.
  • Management Development Program (MDP), Gordon Institute of Business Science (GIBS) (2008).
  • Additional Certifications: Certificate in Mine Environmental Control (COMEC), ISO 45001, SAMTRAC Levels 1-3, NQF Level 5 OHS, SAIOSH & SAIOH certifications (occupational hygiene, hazardous materials, industrial ventilation), and short courses in first aid, firefighting, internal auditing, cybersecurity, and law.

2003 - Dept. of Health, Eastern Cape

Junior Environmental Health Practitioner

Conducted health inspections and community education. Compiled research on the hygiene practices of informal vendors in Buffalo City in conjunction with the municipality's environmental health division.

2004 - 2006 - Anglo Platinum

Ventilation & Occupational Hygiene Trainee, then Technologist

Awarded "Best Trainee" (2004). During the 18-month training, acted in senior roles for approximately 4 months, entrusted to manage the occupational hygiene program for my section. Immediately post-training, appointed Occupational Hygiene Assistant at Amandelbult Concentrator, then promoted to Technologist. Performed risk assessments and environmental stressor testing.

2007 - 2009 - SARCC Metrorail, Gauteng

Safety, Health & Environmental Manager

Revamped SHE programs. Set up an occupational hygiene program from scratch, significantly improving overall OHS performance. NOSA audit scores improved across departments, with more achieving 5-star ratings. Though initially for Wits Region, the program's success led to a national rollout, travelling to Pretoria and Eastern Cape regions.

2008 - 2012 - Yeyethu Trust

Founder & Project Manager

Founded and ran Yeyethu Trust, a community project providing medals and trophies for academic performance in Lencane and Matolweni schools.

2009 - 2010 - Liberty Group Properties

Environment, Health and Safety Manager

Managed SHE programs and construction compliance. Tasked with addressing a DoL investigation following a major incident with fatalities. Successfully implemented DoL-identified controls and a robust OHS management system, minimizing incidents and managing client liability for multi-billion rand contracts (Midlands Mall, Eastgate, Sandton City). This success led to being headhunted by ABSA.

2010 - 2016 - Barclays Africa Group (ABSA)

Environment, Health and Safety Consultant & Risk Specialist

Developed and managed a national asbestos management program, ensuring bank properties were asbestos-free or safely managed. Coordinated safe evacuations for 4,000 employees during critical incidents.

2012 - 2022 - 5G Property Consultants

Managing Director

Founded and ran 5G Property Consultants, providing consultancy in EHS, property management, and compliance across various industries. Clients included Ster-Kinekor, Mine Investment Corporation, Alexander Forbes, and contractors like Masupa Tsela and Vikinduku.

2016 - 2024 - Telkom (CRES) / Gyro Group Pty Ltd

Specialist: Risk, Audit, Business Continuity

Joined Telkom's CRES division, which later became Gyro Group. Instrumental in designing the compliance structure for OHS and risk management, including roles and functions. Upon Gyro's incorporation, took charge of the risk management role, assisting H&S roles in setup and proper functioning. Ensured the risk function operated effectively with no gaps for over two years, even after an executive role was established.

2023 - Present

Author, Writer & Publisher (Self-Employed)

Creating e-learning content and advising on risk and compliance. Authored "Safety First: Essentials for Your OHS Career Journey" and currently working on "Safety First: The Comprehensive Blueprint for Your OHS Career."
